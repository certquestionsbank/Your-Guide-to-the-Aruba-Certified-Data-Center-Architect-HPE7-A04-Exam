## Exam Information

The HPE7-A04 exam is administered by Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) under the Aruba certification track.

- Exam type: Proctored
- Exam duration: 2 hours 30 minutes
- Exam length: 70 questions
- Passing score: 65%
- Delivery languages: English, Japanese, Latin American Spanish

The certification focuses on evaluating the candidate’s ability to design comprehensive network solutions for enterprise data centers, ensuring integration with HPE and Aruba networking technologies.

While the specific objectives are not detailed in the table, the Aruba Certified Data Center Architect exam typically emphasizes the following areas:

**Network Architecture Design:** Understanding core and edge design principles for modern data centers.
Automation and Management Tools: Utilizing Aruba Central and other HPE tools for monitoring and configuration.
**Security and Policy Enforcement:** Implementing Zero Trust and dynamic segmentation models.
**High Availability & Redundancy:** Designing fault-tolerant topologies for mission-critical applications.
**Integration and Virtualization:** Combining physical and virtual infrastructures for efficient resource use.
